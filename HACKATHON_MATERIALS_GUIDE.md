## ğŸš¨ Last-Minute Emergency Guide

**24 Hours Before Deadline - Something Broke!**

1. **Frontend won't start:**
   - Check .env and .env.local exist
   - Delete node_modules and reinstall
   - Check for port conflicts (kill process on 5174)

2. **Contracts not working:**
   - Verify on block explorers
   - Check deployments.json has correct addresses
   - Test with Hardhat scripts first

3. **Video won't upload:**
   - Compress video (use HandBrake)
   - Try different platform (Loom, YouTube, Vimeo)
   - Upload to Google Drive as backup

4. **Form submission errors:**
   - Screenshot everything you've entered
   - Try different browser
   - Clear cache and cookies
   - Contact organizers on Discord

**Remember**: Submit *something* before deadline. You can often update later!
